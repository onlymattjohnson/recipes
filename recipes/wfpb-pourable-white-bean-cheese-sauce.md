# WFPB Cheese Sauce

## Ingredients
* 1 cup white beans (cannellini or navy), rinsed and drained very well
* 1/4 cup raw cashews (soaked in hot water for 10 minutes if your blender isn't high-speed)
* 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil (emulsifies the sauce and boosts turmeric absorption)
* 1/2 to 3/4 cup water (start with 1/2 cup and add more to reach your desired pourability)
* 3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
* 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
* 1 1/2 teaspoons white miso paste
* 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon MSG (start with 1/4; the miso and nutritional yeast already provide heavy umami)
* 1 teaspoon paprika (smoked paprika is excellent here for a "nacho" vibe)
* 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
* 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
* 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric (for that classic yellow cheddar color)
* 1/4 teaspoon lactic acid

## Directions
1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until completely smooth.
2. Adjust with more water until the sauce reaches your desired pourability.
3. Warm gently if you want a hot sauce.
