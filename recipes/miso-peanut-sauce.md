# Miso Peanut Sauce

Heart-healthy peanut sauce with miso, maple, ginger, and sesame.

Source: LLM chat

## Ingredients

* 1/3 cup natural peanut butter (85g)
* 2 tbsp miso paste
* 1 tbsp maple syrup or agave (21g)
* 1 tsp rice wine vinegar (5mL)
* 1/4 tsp ground ginger or freshly minced ginger (0.5g)
* 1/2 tsp sesame seeds (1.5g)
* 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes, optional (0.35g)
* 2 tbsp to 1/4 cup hot water

## Instructions

1. If the peanut butter is thick, microwave it for about 15 seconds.
2. Whisk the miso paste with 2 tbsp hot water until smooth.
3. Whisk in the peanut butter, maple syrup or agave, rice wine vinegar, ginger, sesame seeds, and red pepper flakes.
4. Add more hot water as needed until the sauce reaches the desired consistency.

## Notes

* Serve over noodles, salad, spring rolls, bowls, or vegetables.
