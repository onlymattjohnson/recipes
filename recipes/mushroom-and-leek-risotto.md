# Mushroom and Leek Risotto (Vegan + GF)

Source: [Minimalist Baker](https://minimalistbaker.com/mushroom-and-leek-risotto/)

## Ingredients
- 3 1/2 - 4 cups vegetable broth
- 2 tablespoons olive oil (divided)
- 8 ounces mushrooms (bella, cremini, or shiitake), brushed clean and sliced
- Sea salt and black pepper to taste
- 3/4 cup thinly sliced leeks (well rinsed and dried)
- 1 cup arborio rice
- 1/4 cup dry white wine
- 1 tablespoon vegan butter *(optional)*
- 1/4 cup vegan parmesan cheese
- Fresh chopped parsley *(optional garnish)*

## Instructions

1. Heat vegetable broth in a small saucepan over medium heat, then reduce to low to keep warm.
2. Heat a large saucepan over medium heat. Add half the olive oil and mushrooms, season with salt and pepper, and saute until tender and lightly browned (3-4 minutes). Remove and set aside.
3. Heat the same pan with remaining oil, add leeks, and saute for 1-2 minutes until softened and slightly browned.
4. Add arborio rice and cook for 1 minute, stirring to coat.
5. Pour in white wine and stir gently until liquid is absorbed (1-2 minutes).
6. Gradually add warmed broth in 1/2-cup portions, stirring frequently until rice reaches "al dente" texture (15-20 minutes total).
7. Remove from heat, stir in vegan butter, vegan parmesan, and most of the cooked mushrooms.
8. Taste and adjust seasonings as desired.
9. Divide among bowls, top with remaining mushrooms, additional cheese, and parsley.

**Storage:** Best when fresh; leftovers keep refrigerated for 2-3 days.
