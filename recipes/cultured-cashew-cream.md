# Cultured Cashew Cream

## Ingredients
- 1 cup raw cashews (soaked 2-4 hours or quick soak)
- 1/2 cup water (adjust for thickness)
- 1 1/2 - 2 tablespoons lemon juice (to taste)
- 1-2 teaspoons white miso
- 1/8 - 1/4 teaspoon MSG *(optional but highly recommended)*
- 1/4 teaspoon lactic acid *(optional)*
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder *(optional, subtle savory note)*

## Instructions
1. Blend everything in a high-speed blender until completely smooth.
2. Taste and adjust:
   - More lemon for a brighter flavor
   - More miso for a more cultured flavor
   - A tiny pinch more lactic acid for a more authentic tang
3. Chill for 1-2 hours to thicken and let the flavors settle.
