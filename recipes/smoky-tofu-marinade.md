# Smoky Tofu Marinade

## Ingredients

* 2 tbsp smoky EVOO / smoky "bacon" olive oil
* 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce or tamari
* 2 tsp liquid smoke
* 1 tsp smoked paprika
* 1 tsp garlic powder
* 1 tsp onion powder
* 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 1 tsp maple syrup
* 1/2 tsp cumin
* Pinch of MSG

## Instructions

1. Press tofu for 30 minutes.
2. Tear tofu into chunks.
3. Marinate for 8 hours.
4. Bake at 400°F for 35 minutes, flipping halfway.
